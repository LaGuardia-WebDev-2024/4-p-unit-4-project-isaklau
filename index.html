Isa Lau

Dancer

Isa Lau is a New York City based dancer.

Dancer Biography

17 year old Isa Lau is originally from Queens, New York. She is currently studying dance at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts. Prior to LaGuardia, Isa has studied dance with a concentration in ballet at Ballet Tech, the NYC public school for dance.

Isa has extensive traning in ballet, modern, and contemporary dance, and has experience in hiphop, tap, jazz, and latin dance.

Isa is currently dancing with Flight Path Dance Project for their 6th season.

Works and Creations

Dear... Choreographed and Performed by Isa Lau, Lucia Szell, Kayleigh Griffin, Mia Ganapolsky, Rebecca Adamo (LaGuardia Dance Junior Projects Showcase, 2024)
